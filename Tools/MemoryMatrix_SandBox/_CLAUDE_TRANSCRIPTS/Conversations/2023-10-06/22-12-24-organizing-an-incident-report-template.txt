Here is an outline for an incident report:

Incident Report 

Date/Time of Incident: Friday, October 6, 2023

Location of Incident: 

People Involved: 

Description of Incident:
- What happened? Provide a brief, factual description.

- When did the incident occur? Specify date and time. 

- Where did it happen? Note location and any other relevant details about the setting.

- Who was involved? List names and roles of people involved.

- How did it happen? Explain sequence of events, who did what, any contributing factors.

Injuries/Damage:
- Were there any injuries or damage because of the incident? Describe injuries sustained or damage caused.

- If there were injuries, were first aid or medical treatment provided? Explain.

Corrective Actions: 
- What actions were taken in response to the incident? 

- What will be done to prevent recurrence or improve safety/security? Propose improvements.

- Will any procedures or policies need to be reviewed or revised? Identify anything that needs follow up.

- Photos
- Medical reports 
- Witness statements
- Other supporting documents
