## RELEVANT ALGORITHMS TO RESEARCH

1. **Level of Detail (LOD)** - Game dev concept. More detail up close, less far away
2. **Spatial Hashing** - Quick lookup based on "location" in state space
3. **Hierarchical State Machines** - States within states, each with own context
4. **Event Sourcing** - Compressed history as events, not full snapshots
5. **Context Windowing** - Sliding window of recent relevant data
6. **Semantic Compression** - Summarize meaning, not just truncate
