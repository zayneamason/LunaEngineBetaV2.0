**The honest answer:** You can't truly simulate a LoRA without training it. The whole point is that fine-tuning changes the model's weights in ways that emerge from gradient descent, not from prompting.

### Option 1: Few-Shot Simulation
Take a base Qwen2.5-7B (or even Claude) and stuff the context with 10-15 of our best training examples as few-shot demonstrations. Then test with the same prompts we'd use for evaluation.
