## 1. Venue Management

1.1 Venue Liaison:
   a) Producer B shall serve as the primary liaison with the venue management.
   b) Producer B shall communicate all venue-related information to Producer A in a timely manner.

1.2 Venue Contract:
   a) Producer B shall negotiate the venue contract.
   b) The final venue contract must be approved in writing by both Parties before execution.
   c) Both Parties shall be listed as authorized representatives on the venue contract.

1.3 Setup and Breakdown:
   a) Producer B shall coordinate the venue setup schedule with the venue management.
   b) Producer A shall provide a detailed production schedule to Producer B at least 14 days before the Event.
   c) Both Parties shall collaborate on creating a comprehensive setup and breakdown plan.
   d) Producer B shall oversee the venue setup and breakdown process.

1.4 Insurance and Permits:
   a) Producer B shall obtain all necessary insurance for the venue as required by local and state regulations.
   b) Producer B shall secure all required permits for venue use, including but not limited to:
      - Fire marshal permits
      - Health department permits (if food is served)
      - Noise permits
      - Special event permits
   c) Copies of all insurance policies and permits shall be provided to Producer A at least 7 days before the Event.

1.5 Venue Staffing:
   a) Producer B shall coordinate with the venue to ensure adequate staffing for the Event, including:
      - Security personnel
      - Janitorial staff
      - Technical support staff
   b) Any additional staffing needs must be communicated to Producer A at least 14 days before the Event.

1.6 Venue Facilities:
   a) Producer B shall ensure the venue provides the following facilities in working order:
      - Adequate restroom facilities
      - Proper ventilation and temperature control
      - Accessible entrances and exits
      - Backstage and dressing areas for performers and models
      - VIP areas (if applicable)
   b) Any issues with venue facilities must be promptly communicated to Producer A and resolved before the Event.

## 2. Charity Involvement - "Better Vision for Children"

2.1 Charity Liaison:
   a) Producer B shall serve as the primary liaison with the charity "Better Vision for Children" (the "Charity").
   b) Producer B shall communicate all Charity-related information to Producer A in a timely manner.

2.2 Charity Agreement:
   a) Producer B shall negotiate the agreement with the Charity.
   b) The final Charity agreement must be approved in writing by both Parties before execution.
   c) The agreement shall clearly outline the Charity's involvement in the Event and any financial arrangements.

2.3 Charity Integration:
   a) Both Parties shall collaborate on integrating the Charity's presence into the Event, which may include:
      - Dedicated booth or display area
      - On-stage mentions or presentations
      - Inclusion in Event marketing materials
   b) Producer A shall ensure the Charity's integration aligns with the overall Event production and flow.

2.4 Fundraising Activities:
   a) If applicable, both Parties shall agree on fundraising activities for the Charity, which may include:
      - Silent auctions
      - Donation stations
      - Percentage of ticket sales
   b) Producer B shall coordinate with the Charity on the logistics of any fundraising activities.
   c) Producer A shall integrate fundraising activities into the Event production schedule.

2.5 Financial Transparency:
   a) All financial transactions related to the Charity shall be documented and made available to both Parties.
   b) If the Event is collecting donations on behalf of the Charity, a separate account shall be established for this purpose.
   c) Both Parties shall have visibility into this account.

2.6 Post-Event Reporting:
   a) Producer B shall coordinate with the Charity to provide a post-Event report detailing:
      - Total funds raised
      - Charity's impact at the Event
      - Attendee engagement with the Charity
   b) This report shall be provided to both Parties within 14 days after the Event.

3.3 Decision Making:
