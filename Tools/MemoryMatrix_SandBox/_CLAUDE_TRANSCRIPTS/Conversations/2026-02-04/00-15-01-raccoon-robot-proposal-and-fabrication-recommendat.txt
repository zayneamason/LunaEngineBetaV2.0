[ASSISTANT] 2026-02-04T00:22:52.546675Z
 Ah, got it - you want the fabrication breakdown explained simply for the panel so they understand *how* it gets built, not just what it is.

---

**How We Build LUNA**

**The Skeleton (Steel Cage)**
We weld steel tubes together to make LUNA's bones. This is her frame - strong enough to hold everything inside and survive the desert.

**The Skin (Sheet Metal + CNC Ribs)**
Thin metal sheets wrap around the outside like armor. We cut curved pieces called "ribs" on a computer-controlled machine (CNC) so they fit perfectly. These ribs bolt onto the skeleton and give LUNA her shape - like how your ribs give your chest its shape.

**The Face (Sculpted Foam)**
The nose and face details are carved from foam - it's light and easy to shape into cute raccoon features. The nose pops off like a lid so we can get inside to fix things or charge batteries.

**The Guts (Batteries + Computer + Motors)**
Inside the body:
- **Batteries** - like a really big phone battery, gives her 10+ hours of roaming
- **Computer brain** - a small computer that runs her AI so she can listen and respond
- **Motors** - spin the wheels so she can move around on her own
- **Speakers + Microphone** - so she can hear you and talk back

**The Tail (LED Strips + Flexible Structure)**
Wire frame wrapped in diffusion material with LED strips inside. Changes colors based on the conversation mood.

**The Wheels (Rocket Style)**
Off-the-shelf wheels modified with custom hub covers we fabricate to look like little rockets. Bolted to motor mounts welded to the base frame.

---

**Assembly Order:**
1. Weld steel cage frame
2. Cut and attach CNC ribs
3. Wrap sheet metal skin, fasten to ribs
4. Mount motors and wheels to base
5. Install battery tray and electronics
6. Wire everything up
7. Attach foam nose/face (removable)
8. Install tail
9. Test and seal all gaps against dust/salt

---

**Tools We're Using:**
- MIG welder (steel frame)
- CNC router (ribs, brackets)
- Grinders and finishing tools
- Basic hand tools for assembly
- Foam carving tools (hot wire, rasps)
- Soldering station (electronics)
