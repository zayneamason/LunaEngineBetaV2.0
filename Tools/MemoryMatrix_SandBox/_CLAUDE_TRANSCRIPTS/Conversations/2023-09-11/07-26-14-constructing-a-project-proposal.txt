Here is an outline for a project proposal:

Title Page
- Title of proposed project
- Your name and contact information 

Executive Summary (1 page)
- Brief overview of the project, including the problem statement, objectives, methods and expected outcomes.

Problem Statement (1 page)  
- Describe the specific problem or opportunity the project will address.
- Provide background context such as industry trends, client needs, or market analysis that demonstrates the need for this project.

Objectives (1/2 page)
- List the main goals and deliverables of the project.
- Explain how the project will address the problem statement.

Methods (1-2 pages)
- Outline the overall approach and key activities involved in executing the project. 
- Provide details on data collection tools, analytical techniques, technologies or other methods used.

Expected Outcomes (1/2 - 1 page)
- Predict and quantify the expected results and benefits of completing the project.
- Explain the value of these outcomes to the client/stakeholders.

Timeline (1/2 page) 
- Provide a high-level timeline of key project milestones and estimated completion dates.

Budget (1 page)
- Estimate total budget required including a breakdown of costs (labor, equipment, travel, etc).

Conclusion (1 paragraph)
- Summarize the feasibility and benefits of undertaking this project.

I hope this outline provides a helpful starting point. Let me know if you would like me to expand on any section in more detail.
