**Integration points are tricky:**

4. **Testing** — no agent can verify their piece works without the others

**The "later Luna" flow especially:**
- Touches `state.py` → `api.py` → `memory_log.py` → `launcher.py`
- One agent's assumptions about another's interface = bugs
