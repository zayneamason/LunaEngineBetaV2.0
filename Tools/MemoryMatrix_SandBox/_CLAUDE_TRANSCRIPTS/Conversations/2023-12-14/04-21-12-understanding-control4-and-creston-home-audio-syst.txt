What is control4 and creston related to audio equipment?

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[ASSISTANT] 2023-12-14T04:21:37.205265Z
 Control4 and Creston are home automation and smart home control systems that can integrate various components like lighting, audio/video, security, HVAC, etc. into one unified system that can be controlled remotely. Some key points about them related to audio equipment:

- Both Control4 and Creston can integrate audio components like receivers, speakers, TVs, streaming devices into their systems and allow centralized control of the entire audio system from tablets/phones or in-wall touchscreens.

- They support leading brands of audio gear and allow advanced audio functionality like multi-room audio, volume control, source selection, etc. to be automated and voice controlled.

- With the audio integration, you can do things like play the same music or audio source in multiple zones of the home simultaneously. Or control which speakers play at what volume from a single interface.

- They provide options to add key audio components like amplifiers, matrix switchers, and multi-channel receivers/processors as part of the automation system.

- High-end Control4 and Creston systems include advanced features on the audio side like acoustic room calibration, equalization, bass management, and dynamic range compression across multiple zones.

In summary, Control4 and Creston are whole-home automation ecosystems that have deep integration with audio sources and systems, bringing centralized, intelligent control and advanced functionality to the table. This sets them apart from basic remote controls in terms of audio management capabilities.
