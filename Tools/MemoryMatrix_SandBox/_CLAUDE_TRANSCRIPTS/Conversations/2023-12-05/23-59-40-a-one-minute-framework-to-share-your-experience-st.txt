[ASSISTANT] 2023-12-05T23:59:59.713795Z
Here is a suggested framework for a concise yet compelling "tell me about yourself" response for a job interview:

"I started my career in [field / industry] over 10 years ago as a [job title]. In that role, I [key achievement or responsibility]. From there, I moved on to [next position], gaining experience in [area that relates to target role]. Most recently, I've been working as a [current title] and have developed strengths in [top 3 relevant skills - e.g. analysis, client relations, project management]. 

All of these experiences have allowed me to build a strong foundation of [industy] knowledge and honed skills like [skill 1, skill 2, skill 3] that I'm excited to leverage in this [target job title] role with your company. If hired, I know I can make an immediate impact by [ways you can add value].

On a personal level, I'm passionate about [interests or goals] and I bring positive energy and strong motivation to everything I do. I'm really drawn to this opportunity because [reasons role / company excites you]."

In about a minute, this covers past experience, relevant strengths and value you can add, along with a glimpse into what motivates you - all important areas for an interviewer to understand who you are and what makes you a strong fit.
