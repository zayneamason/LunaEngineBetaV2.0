Here is a concise list of the DaVinci Resolve keyboard shortcuts:

Playback Controls:
- Play/Pause: Space or K  
- Fast Forward: L
- Fast Reverse: J
- Step Forward: Right Arrow  
- Step Reverse: Left Arrow

Timeline Navigation:  
- Go to Start: Home
- Go to End: End
- Go to In Point: I
- Go to Out Point: O  

Editing:
- Blade: B
- Trim: T
- Add Transition: Ctrl+T
- Cut: Ctrl+X
- Copy: Ctrl+C
- Paste: Ctrl+V
- Undo: Ctrl+Z
- Redo: Ctrl+Shift+Z

Selection:
- Select All: Ctrl+A
- Deselect All: Ctrl+Shift+A 
- Select Clip: Shift+V

View:
- Zoom In: =
- Zoom Out: -
