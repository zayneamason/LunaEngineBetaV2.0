- Background in linguistics and formal languages
- Saw programs as *assertions about state*—preconditions, postconditions, invariants
- Could translate messy intuition into precise logical statements
- Core insight: if you can't specify it formally, you don't understand it

**The common thread:**

All three had deep **mathematical maturity**—not just knowing techniques, but thinking in terms of proof, invariants, and abstraction. They also wrote extensively, forcing clarity.
