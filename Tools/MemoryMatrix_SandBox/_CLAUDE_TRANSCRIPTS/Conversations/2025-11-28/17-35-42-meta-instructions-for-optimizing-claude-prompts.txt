## What "Decay" Actually Means

Not everything should be remembered equally over time. The system uses **power-law forgetting** (how human memory actually works):

- **Yesterday's conversation** → 90% retrievable
- **Last week's conversation** → 60% retrievable  
- **Last month's conversation** → 30% retrievable
- **6 months ago** → 10% retrievable (unless you keep accessing it)

**But some things never decay:**
- Your name, role, preferences
- Your voice profile
- Anything you explicitly pin as "always remember"

**And accessing something boosts its stability** — if you keep referencing that fabrication project, it stays fresh.

For your Post Generator / Content Pipeline:

## The Memory System in This Context

NOW the three-tier architecture makes sense:

| Tier | Companion Use |
|------|---------------|
| **Hot** | Current conversation, active task, immediate context |
| **Warm** | Recent projects, ongoing preferences, this week's patterns |
| **Cold** | Long-term relationship history, who you are, what you care about |

The 1MB budget question becomes: **How much does a companion need to remember to feel continuous?**

Not much, actually — humans maintain relationships with way less. It's about remembering *the right things*, not everything.
