 The word you're looking for is idealization or idealizing someone.

Idealization refers to the cognitive process of attributing unrealistically positive or exaggerated qualities to someone, usually in the context of infatuation or being attracted to them. When you idealize someone, you mentally construct an image of them that is embellished and disconnected from reality, focusing selectively on their positive traits while ignoring or downplaying any flaws or negative aspects.

This phenomenon often occurs in the early stages of romantic attraction, where the object of infatuation is put on a pedestal and seen through rose-tinted glasses. The idealized version built up in one's mind bears little resemblance to the real, more multidimensional person. It's a form of fantasy thinking driven by intense feelings rather than objective reality.

So in summary, idealization or idealizing captures the tendency to obsess over someone's perceived perfection and build up an unrealistic, overly positive mental representation of them that diverges significantly from who they truly are.
