Best mixture for vinegar based cleaning spray for black rock city

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[ASSISTANT] 2024-08-21T23:32:44.535699Z
 For a vinegar-based cleaning spray suitable for use in Black Rock City (the location of Burning Man), I'd suggest the following mixture:

1. 1 part white vinegar
2. 1 part water
3. A few drops of liquid dish soap (biodegradable if possible)
4. Optional: 10-15 drops of essential oil (like tea tree, lavender, or lemon) for fragrance

This mixture is effective for general cleaning and is environmentally friendly, which is important for the "Leave No Trace" principles followed at Burning Man. The vinegar acts as a natural disinfectant and deodorizer, while the dish soap helps to break down dirt and grime.

A few things to keep in mind for Black Rock City:

1. Use a spray bottle that seals well to prevent leaks in your gear.
2. The alkaline dust in the playa can neutralize the acidity of the vinegar, so you might need to use it more liberally than usual.
3. Avoid spraying this solution on playa dust directly, as it can create mud. Instead, use it on hard surfaces or fabric items.
4. Remember to pack out all cleaning materials and dirty water, adhering to Leave No Trace principles.
