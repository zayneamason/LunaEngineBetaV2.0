  - Logistics and transportation
  - Construction/physical assets

- Include non-dilution provisions if appropriate

- Create an Operating Agreement that reflects your roles

# FOUNDER AGREEMENT

This Founder Agreement (the "Agreement") is made effective as of [DATE] by and between the following parties:

## ARTICLE 1: PARTIES

1.1. [FOUNDER FULL NAME], an individual residing at [ADDRESS] ("Founding Member")
1.2. [OTHER FOUNDER(S) FULL NAME(S)], individuals residing at [ADDRESS(ES)] (collectively with Founding Member, the "Founders")

## ARTICLE 2: COMPANY FORMATION

2.1. The Founders agree to form a limited liability company (the "Company") under the laws of [STATE].
2.2. The Company's primary business purpose shall be [BRIEF DESCRIPTION OF BUSINESS].
2.3. The Founders agree to complete LLC formation within [NUMBER] days of signing this Agreement.

## ARTICLE 3: FOUNDING MEMBER'S ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

3.1. Position and Title
- Primary Role: Chief Technical Officer (CTO) and Creative Director
- Reports to: Board of Directors

3.2. Key Responsibilities
The Founding Member shall be responsible for:

Technical Leadership:
- Web design and development
- Application and tool development
- Technical consultation and knowledge management
- Digital asset management
- Media system architecture

Creative Direction:
- Brand development and management
- Marketing strategy
- Photography and videography
- Social media management
- IP management

Operational Support:
- Construction project management
- Logistics coordination
- Asset security and recovery
- Transportation management
- Business development

## ARTICLE 4: CAPITAL CONTRIBUTIONS

4.1. Initial Contributions
The Founding Member agrees to contribute:
- Initial capital: $[AMOUNT]
- Equipment and assets: [LIST OF SIGNIFICANT ITEMS]
- Intellectual property: [LIST OF IP BEING CONTRIBUTED]

4.2. Valuation
The agreed-upon valuation of non-cash contributions:
- Equipment and assets: $[AMOUNT]
- Intellectual property: $[AMOUNT]

## ARTICLE 5: OWNERSHIP AND EQUITY

5.1. Equity Distribution
- Founding Member's initial ownership: [X]% of the Company
- Equity vesting schedule: [DETAILS OF VESTING PERIOD]

5.2. Vesting Schedule
- Cliff period: [LENGTH OF CLIFF]
- Vesting period: [TOTAL VESTING PERIOD]
- Vesting frequency: [MONTHLY/QUARTERLY]

## ARTICLE 6: INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

6.1. Prior IP
- All intellectual property created by Founding Member prior to this Agreement remains their property
- Company receives perpetual, royalty-free license to use prior IP for business purposes

6.2. New IP
- All intellectual property created during employment belongs to the Company
- Founding Member retains right to be credited as creator

## ARTICLE 7: COMPENSATION

7.1. Initial Compensation
- Base salary: $[AMOUNT]/year once company is funded
- Performance bonus: [TERMS]
- Benefits: [LIST OF BENEFITS]

7.2. Future Compensation
- Salary review period: [FREQUENCY]
- Profit sharing: [TERMS]

## ARTICLE 8: DECISION MAKING

8.1. Voting Rights
- Major decisions require [UNANIMOUS/MAJORITY] approval
- Founding Member has voting power proportional to ownership stake

8.2. Protected Decisions
The following decisions require Founding Member's explicit approval:
- Sale or merger of the Company
- Additional fundraising rounds
- Changes to core business model
- Modification of Founding Member's roles

## ARTICLE 9: EXIT AND TERMINATION

9.1. Voluntary Departure
- Notice period: [LENGTH]
- Treatment of vested equity
- Non-compete terms

9.2. Involuntary Termination
- Severance terms
- Equity treatment
- Causes for termination

## ARTICLE 10: CONFIDENTIALITY

10.1. The Founding Member agrees to:
- Maintain confidentiality of Company information
- Return all Company property upon departure
- Not disclose trade secrets or proprietary information

## ARTICLE 11: DISPUTE RESOLUTION

11.1. Resolution Process
- Good faith negotiation
- Mediation
- Binding arbitration in [JURISDICTION]
