- Create tiered sponsorship levels

  * Photo opportunities

4. Sponsor Outreach
- Research potential sponsors aligned with:

  * Budget range
  * Geographic location

- Make initial contacts
- Schedule presentations

5. Timeline Management
- Create backward timeline including:
  * Sponsorship procurement deadline
  * Funding receipt date
