FCOB (Flip-Chip-On-Board) strips are different from traditional SMD5050 WS2814 strips:

**FCOB Advantages:**
- **Smoother light** - no visible individual LED dots/hotspots
- **Higher density** - more LEDs per meter (typically 784 LEDs/m vs 60-144 LEDs/m for SMD)
- **Better heat dissipation** 
- **More uniform lighting** - looks like a continuous light strip

**For Controllers:**
The same recommendations apply - WLED ESP32 controllers work great with FCOB WS2814 strips. In fact, several Amazon reviews I found specifically mentioned successful setups with FCOB WS2814 strips and WLED controllers.

**Power Considerations:**
FCOB strips typically draw similar power to SMD versions, but the higher LED density means potentially higher total power consumption for the same length. Make sure to calculate power needs based on your actual LED count (should be on the packaging).

Your FCOB strips will give you much better visual results than traditional SMD strips - they're perfect for accent lighting, cove lighting, or anywhere you want smooth, continuous illumination without visible LED dots.
