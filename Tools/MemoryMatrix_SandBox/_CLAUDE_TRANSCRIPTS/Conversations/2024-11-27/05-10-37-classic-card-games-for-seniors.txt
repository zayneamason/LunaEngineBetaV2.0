# Ultimate Card Fusion

## Players
3-6 players

## Equipment
- Two standard 52-card decks with jokers
- Cribbage board or scoring pad

## Game Overview
Ultimate Card Fusion combines elements of Rummy, Gin, Cribbage, Hearts, Spades, and Bid Whist into a strategic multi-round game. Each round alternates between different phases inspired by classic card games.

## Gameplay Phases

### Phase 1: The Bid (from Bid Whist)
- Players bid on how many "tricks" they can win in the later phases
- The highest bidder becomes the "Captain" for the round
- The Captain chooses the trump suit

### Phase 2: The Meld (from Rummy/Gin)
- Players are dealt 10 cards each
- Players try to form sets and runs
- A special "gin bonus" is awarded for completing all melds
- Unmatched cards become penalty points

### Phase 3: The Hunt (from Hearts/Spades)
- Using remaining cards, players play a trick-taking round
- Hearts score negative points (like Hearts)
- Spades are always trump (like Spades)
- The Captain must meet their bid or face penalties

### Phase 4: The Crib (from Cribbage)
- Each player contributes two cards to a community crib
- Players score points based on combinations:
  - Fifteens (15 points)
  - Pairs
  - Runs
  - Flushes

### Scoring
- Melding points (Phase 2): 10 points per set/run
- Gin bonus: 25 points
- Trick points (Phase 3): 10 points per trick
- Meeting bid: 20 point bonus
- Hearts taken: -5 points each
- Cribbage combinations (Phase 4): Standard cribbage scoring
- First player to 500 points wins

## Special Rules

### Jokers
- Act as wild cards in melding phase
- Count as highest trump in trick-taking phase
- Worth 20 points in cribbage combinations

### Solitaire Element
- Between rounds, players can attempt a "Solitaire Challenge"
- Success grants bonus points for next round
- Failure results in penalty points

### Strategy Tips
1. Balance bidding ambition with realistic capabilities
2. Save high trumps for critical tricks
3. Watch for opportunities to block opponents' melds
4. Consider contributing strong cards to crib when holding Captain position

This game rewards diverse skills:
- Bidding judgment (Bid Whist)
- Pattern matching (Rummy/Gin)
- Trick management (Hearts/Spades)
- Combination counting (Cribbage)
- Risk assessment (Solitaire challenge)
