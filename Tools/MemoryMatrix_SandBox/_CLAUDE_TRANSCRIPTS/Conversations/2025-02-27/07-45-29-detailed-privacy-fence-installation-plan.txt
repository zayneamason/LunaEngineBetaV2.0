 # Front Yard Privacy Fence Project Description

The front yard privacy fence project will span approximately 34 feet across the front of the house. Nine square tubing posts, each 1.5 inches in diameter, will be installed using a skid with auger attachment. These posts will support eight 16-gauge steel sheets, each measuring 4 feet by 8 feet. The metal sheets will be attached to the posts by welding them to angle iron tabs on the posts. After installation, the fence will receive a patina treatment to create a solid rust appearance, followed by an exterior clear rust protectant sealer for durability.

# Connecting Wind Barrier to Stairway

The connecting wind barrier will feature a support structure of four posts with welded base plates. The side posts will be secured to the cement wall through basement subframing using lag bolts. The middle posts will be installed with Simpson concrete anchors and epoxy. All hardware for this section will utilize the Simpson Strong-Tie concrete fastening system to ensure stability and longevity.
