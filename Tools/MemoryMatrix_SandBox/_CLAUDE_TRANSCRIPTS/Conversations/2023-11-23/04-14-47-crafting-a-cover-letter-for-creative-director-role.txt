Here is an effective cover letter outline:

Header: Your contact information (name, address, phone number, email) at the top 

Date
Name/Title of hiring manager/recruiter 
Company name
Company address

Opening paragraph:
- Briefly state the position you are applying for and where you found the job posting
- 1-2 sentences on why you are interested in the role/company

Middle paragraphs:
- Highlight 2-3 relevant qualifications/skills/experiences that match the job description 
- Give specific examples of times you demonstrated those skills (situation-task-result)
- Align your value proposition to what they are looking for 
- Convey why you are a great culture add to the team

Closing paragraph: 
- Summarize top strengths that would allow you to excel in the position  
- Express appreciation and enthusiasm for being considered 
- Include proactive next steps and provide your contact information 

Signature with typed full name

Key Tips:
- Assert confidence but not arrogance 
- Show rather than tell your fit  
- Mirror company's voice and values  
- Check for typos, grammar issues, factual accuracy
- Use active voice and compelling verbs

I'm happy to provide examples or refine your cover letter outline/structure. Let me know if you have any other questions!
