- Puddles - An ironic name for a Tuttle who is terrified of actual puddles. She carefully tip-toes around any small pools of water and wears galoshes even when it's sunny outside. 

- Raindrop - A nervous Tuttle who was traumatized as a child when a drop of water fell on his head. He now ducks and covers whenever he hears thunder or sees dark rain clouds. He wears a rain hat with a tiny umbrella attached.  

- Dewey - He enjoys mornings but panics if he notices dew glistening on the grass. He'll hop around trying not to let his fur get damp. He carries a tiny hairdryer just in case.
