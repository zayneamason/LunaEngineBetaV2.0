Here are some creative private label product ideas for boats, fishing, and water recreation under $10 to manufacture with a potential $30 profit margin:

- Custom printed/designed fish stringers - Basic stringers could be manufactured overseas very cheaply, then you could sell custom printed or themed versions (favorite sports teams, funny sayings, etc.) at a premium.

- Boat deck grip tape - Similar to skateboard grip tape but designed for boat decks to add traction. Could be customized with different colors/designs.

- Fishing lure storage cases - Hard or soft cases to store/organize fishing lures and tackle, with custom branded designs.

- Floating sunglass straps - Simple product but you could create trendy/fun strap designs to match certain boat aesthetics. 

- Custom anchor buoy balls - Fun marker balls that attach to boat anchors to easily spot them in the water. Manufacture plain ones overseas then customize colors and printing.

- Vinyl boat decals/stickers - Endless design options for custom branded boat names, sea creatures, dive flags, etc. that could retail at a nice markup.

- Waterproof phone pouches - Basic pouches you could then print/brand with unique colors and designs. Useful for boating and watersports.

- Premium boat fenders - Import basic fenders then upgrade part of the production with custom colors/prints and market as a higher-end brand. 

I tried to focus on relatively simple products that would be easy and affordable to manufacture, don't require large custom molds, and allow you to add value through custom design. Let me know if you need any other suggestions!

[ASSISTANT] 2023-11-30T23:18:10.372510Z
Here are some product ideas for boats that help address environmental factors like rain and sun without needing customization:

Boat Covers - Manufacture basic boat covers overseas using waterproof and UV-resistant fabric. Focus marketing on protection from the elements. Could have a few standard sizes with adjustable features. 

Deck Awnings - Retractable shade awnings that attach to railings to provide shelter from sun and light rain. Market to boaters and water recreators.

Waterproof Storage Bags - Bags designed to keep contents dry even when bag gets wet. Market for boating, beach, rainy activities. Could have clear fronts to see contents.

Non-Slip Deck Pads - Cushioned pads with grippy bottom to place on boat seats and decks for stability even when wet. Help prevent slips and falls. 

Waterproof Phone Cases - Simple cases to protect phones from rain, splashes, and accidental drops in water. Useful for many sports.

Drying Racks - Racks and rigging to hang towels, clothes and gear to dry more quickly. Appeal to boaters, campers, outdoor markets.

With these types of products, you can take advantage of overseas manufacturing without needing customization for each order. The focus is on their utility for outdoor activities rather than fashion or custom looks. Let me know if you need any other ideas in this direction!
