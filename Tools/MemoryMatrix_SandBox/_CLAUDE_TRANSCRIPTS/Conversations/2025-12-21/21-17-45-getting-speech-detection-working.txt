## Audio Format & Buffering Strategy

**Why 64ms chunks @ 16kHz:**
- 16kHz = good enough for voice (speech lives ~8kHz, Nyquist at 8kHz)
- 64ms = ~latency sweet spot (too small = overhead, too large = laggy)
- Mono = saves bandwidth

**Buffering logic in Hub:**
```
Chunk arrives → VAD process (instant)
              → If voice active: buffer for Whisper
              → Whisper processes accumulated chunks
              → Output partial transcripts as it goes
              → Clear buffer on silence (VAD confidence < 0.3)
```
