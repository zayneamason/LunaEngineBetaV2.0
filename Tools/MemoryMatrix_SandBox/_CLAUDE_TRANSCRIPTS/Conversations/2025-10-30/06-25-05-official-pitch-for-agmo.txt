# AGMO: The AI Governance Metrics Observatory

## Official Pitch

**The AI Governance Metrics Observatory (AGMO)** provides independent, data-driven analysis of AI governance structures worldwide. Through rigorous quantitative research, we track how AI policy decisions are made, who influences them, and whether governance systems effectively serve broad societal interests.

### The Problem

AI governance is opaque. Policymakers, journalists, and researchers lack quantitative metrics to assess:
- Whether interventions are working
- Who actually influences policy decisions
- If we're approaching irreversible technological lock-in
- How power concentrates in AI development

### The Solution

AGMO provides the first comprehensive, quantitative measurement system for global AI governance, tracking:

- **Regulatory Capture Coefficient C(t)** - Measuring alignment between regulatory outputs and industry preferences
- **Competitive Pressure Index R(t)** - Tracking market concentration and barriers to entry
- **Lock-in Coefficient L(t)** - Assessing irreversibility of current trajectories
- **AI Power Accumulation P_AI(t)** - Quantifying concentration of control over AI systems

### Our Approach

✓ **Rigorous data collection** - Systematic analysis of policy documents, regulatory proceedings, and institutional behavior  
✓ **Transparent methodology** - All methods peer-reviewed and publicly documented  
✓ **Open data** - Raw data and tools available for independent verification  
✓ **Non-partisan analysis** - We measure reality, not ideology

### Deliverables

- **Quarterly reports** with current governance measurements
- **Real-time public dashboard** tracking key metrics
- **Peer-reviewed publications** establishing methodological standards
- **Policy recommendations** based on empirical evidence

### Why This Matters

As AI systems integrate into critical infrastructure, governance decisions being made today may become irreversible. We need measurement infrastructure to understand whether our institutions are functioning effectively—before it's too late.

### Strategic Positioning

AGMO doesn't announce itself as an activist organization. We're academics and researchers who happen to find evidence about power dynamics. **We let the data speak**—and when capture coefficients reach 0.8, the numbers are damning enough without editorial commentary.

### Year 1 Goals

- Launch with validated measurement framework
- Publish 4 quarterly reports
- Establish real-time public dashboard
- Secure academic credibility through peer review
- Become cited source for policymakers and journalists

### Contact

**Website:** agmo.org  
**Email:** director@agmo.org

*"Measuring the systems that govern the most powerful technology in human history."*
