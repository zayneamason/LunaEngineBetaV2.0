### The Core Equation

```
L(t) = [E(t) + I(t) + D(t)] / 3
```

Three components, averaged:

| Component | What It Measures |
|-----------|------------------|
| **E(t)** - Economic Integration | How much the economy *depends* on the system |
| **I(t)** - Institutional Dependence | How much government/institutions *require* it |
| **D(t)** - Deployment/Infrastructure | How much physical infrastructure is *built around* it |

Each ranges from 0 to 1. The average gives you L(t).

| L(t) Value | What It Means |
