 The image you're referring to isn't showing in the email because it appears to be an attachment that hasn't been loaded or displayed yet. I can see in the email content a line that says:

"DynamicDisplayCreative_.jpg"

This suggests there's an image file attached to the email with that filename, but it's not being displayed inline in the email body. Instead, you're seeing the filename as text.

There could be a few reasons for this:

1. The email client may be set to not automatically display images.
2. The image might not have finished downloading yet.
3. There could be an issue with how the image was attached to the email.

To view the image, you might need to:
- Check your email settings to allow images to display automatically
- Click on the attachment to open it separately
- Wait a moment to see if it loads after the text content

If you're still unable to see the image after trying these steps, you may want to contact the sender to make sure the image was attached correctly.
