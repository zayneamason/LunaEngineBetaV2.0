[ASSISTANT] 2024-08-07T08:10:06.548455Z
 Here's a step-by-step guide based on the content provided:

1. Prepare your rolling surface, such as a rolling tray.

2. Prepare your cannabis:
   - Break apart the cannabis by hand rather than using a grinder.
   - Remove any stems and seeds.
   - Use approximately 1.75 grams of cannabis (minimum 1.5 grams).

3. Load the cannabis into the rolling machine:
   - Place the cannabis in the center of the machine.
   - Use your hands to spread it evenly.
   - Push the cannabis to one side, matching the length of your wrap.

4. Close the machine and roll it once to shape the cannabis.

5. Begin inserting the blunt wrap:
   - Slowly feed the wrap into the machine.
   - Leave enough paper out to lick.

6. Lick the edge of the wrap and roll it into the machine to seal.

7. Open the machine and remove the rolled blunt.

8. Seal the blunt:
   - Lick and stick the wrap to ensure it's properly sealed.

9. Prepare a filter tip (optional but recommended):
   - Roll a tip that's bigger and looser than usual.

10. Insert the filter tip:
    - Start at the looser end of the blunt.
    - Gently push it in, which will also help pack the blunt.

11. Finish sealing the blunt wrap, securing the filter tip in place.

12. Pack any loose cannabis:
    - Use your fingertip to pack cannabis into the open end of the blunt.

13. Even out the filter tip if necessary.

14. Your blunt is now ready to smoke.

Remember to use this information responsibly and in accordance with local laws and regulations.
