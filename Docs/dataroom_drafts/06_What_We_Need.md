# What We Need

## What We're Building Toward

A working sovereign AI deployed in a real community by Q4 2026. Not a prototype. Not a pitch deck. A system that preserves knowledge, supports governance, and runs without internet -- in the hands of people who need it.

The engine works. The partnerships are signed. The community is ready. What's missing is the bridge between here and there.

## What We Need

**Advisory:** People who've built technology for underserved markets. People who understand grant funding, social enterprise, and the difference between a product roadmap and a community relationship. We don't need advice on how to scale -- we need advice on how to stay grounded while we grow.

**Funding:** $70K-$125K for Year 1. This covers engineering, the Kinoni ICT Hub build-out, first-cohort programming, and operations. We're pursuing grants and institutional partnerships, not venture capital. The funding model has to match the values.

**Technical Partnerships:** On-device inference optimization. Low-resource language model work (Luganda, Swahili). Solar-powered edge computing. If you're building for the margins, we should be talking.

**Implementation Partnerships:** Organizations working with indigenous communities, rural education programs, cultural preservation initiatives. Luna is infrastructure -- it needs people on the ground who already have trust.

## How to Engage

There are multiple seats at this table:

- **Advisor:** Join the advisory network. Monthly check-ins, strategic guidance, introductions.
- **Funder:** Grant funding, program funding, or direct investment in The Forge (implementation services).
- **Technical Partner:** Collaborate on specific technical challenges (language models, edge computing, offline-first architecture).
- **Implementation Partner:** Bring Luna to a community you already serve.

## What We Offer

Early position in sovereign AI -- not as a market category, but as a fundamental shift in how technology relates to the people who use it.

Access to communities that corporate AI will never serve. Not because those communities aren't valuable, but because the value can't be extracted. Luna is built for the places where extraction isn't an option and ownership isn't negotiable.
