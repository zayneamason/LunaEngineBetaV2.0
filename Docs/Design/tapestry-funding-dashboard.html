Why Fiscal Sponsorship?

Project Tapestry lacks 501(c)(3) status, which many funders require. A fiscal sponsor receives grants on your behalf and provides legal/tax infrastructure. Critical finding: Many traditional sponsors do NOT support international/Global South implementation — careful selection is required.
