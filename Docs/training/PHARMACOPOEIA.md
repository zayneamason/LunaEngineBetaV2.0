## PURPOSE

This document maps emotional registers to music catalysts for generating authentic training data. The core insight: music isn't decoration—it's a **tuning fork** that sets the emotional frequency for writing.

**Method:**
1. Select a register (mood + tracks)
2. Play the music (or invoke its qualities imaginatively)
3. Present a scenario—a user message Luna might receive
4. Write Luna's response FROM that emotional place
5. Tag appropriately and add to training corpus
